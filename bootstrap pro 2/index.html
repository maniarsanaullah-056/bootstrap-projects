Namz

Namaz (Salah) is the daily prayer in Islam. It is performed five times a day to worship Allah and seek His guidance. Namaz teaches discipline, patience, and devotion. It is a way to stay close to Allah, ask for forgiveness, and express gratitude for His blessings.

moreinfomation
DUA

Dua is a prayer to Allah. It is a way to ask for help, guidance, forgiveness, and blessings. It strengthens faith and brings peace to the heart.

moreinfomation
IMPORTANCE OF ZAKAT

Zakat is one of the five pillars of Islam. It is a mandatory charity given by Muslims to help the poor and needy. Zakat purifies wealth, teaches generosity, and promotes social justice. By giving Zakat, Muslims show gratitude to Allah and support the welfare of the community.

moreinfomation
FAST(ROZA)

Roza (fasting) is an important act of worship in Islam. Muslims fast during the month of Ramadan from dawn to sunset. Fasting teaches patience, self-control, and empathy for the poor. It is a way to purify the soul, increase faith, and seek Allah’s blessings and forgiveness.

moreinformation
HAJJ

Hajj is an important pillar of Islam and a pilgrimage to the holy city of Mecca. Every adult Muslim who is physically and financially able must perform Hajj at least once in their lifetime. During Hajj, Muslims perform rituals like Tawaf, Sa’i, and standing at Arafat. Hajj teaches equality, patience, and devotion to Allah. It is a way to seek forgiveness and strengthen faith.
